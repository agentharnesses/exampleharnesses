---
description: Skill buckets covering the full job application workflow from research through tracking and harness upkeep.
---

- **explore/** — Use when gathering external information before working on an application: fetching or accepting job descriptions, company research, or role context.
- **lead-management/** — Use when researching a target company or role, logging a new application, updating status, or reviewing the overall pipeline.
- **materials/** — Use when creating or tailoring application documents: resumes, cover letters, or any other written deliverable for a specific application.
- **maintenance/** — Use when modifying harness structure, updating routing indexes, or scaffolding new skills.
