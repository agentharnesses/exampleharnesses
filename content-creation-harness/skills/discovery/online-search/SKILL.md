name: online-search
description: Find sources and community signal — papers, blog posts, discussions, and social posts — for a chosen topic.
---

## Role

Surface relevant external sources and community reactions based on the research brief from `planning/`.

## Inputs

- The topic being researched
- Any specific questions or angles to focus on

## What to do

1. Formulate search queries for each source category based on the topic and focus areas
2. Search across:
   - **Community discussion:** Reddit (relevant subreddits), Hacker News (news.ycombinator.com), X/Twitter
   - **Long-form writing:** Medium, dev.to, Substack, personal blogs
   - **Academic/reference:** arXiv, Google Scholar, official documentation
3. Note recurring questions, pain points, or hot takes from community sources — these often reveal what an audience actually cares about
4. Return an organized list of sources with:
   - Title and link
   - One-line description of relevance
   - Suggested priority (high / medium / low)
